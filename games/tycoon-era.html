What's in the game

🏢

8 business tiers

Lemonade Stand → Food Truck → Coffee Shop → Restaurant → Hotel → Cruise → Airline → Space Tourism. Each unlocks at a real lifetime-earnings threshold.

🤖

Hire managers

Automate any business once it pays for its own manager. Then it earns while you're away, capped fairly and never abusively.

💤

Offline catchup

Earn while the app is closed at 25% of your normal rate, capped by the offline duration. Upgrade the cap as you grow.

📈

Stock market

Once unlocked, trade parody-named tickers with real volatility curves. A side-game for cash diversification.

⚡

Hero boosts

10× tap multiplier for 30 seconds. Earn them through ad watches or upgrade to the Pass for charges-based access.

🌌

Multi-era arc

Era 1 is live. Eras 2-6 are in design — each adds new tiers, new mechanics, and a fresh progression curve when you've maxed out.

What makes it different

Most idle games gate progression behind energy systems, FOMO timers, or aggressive paywalls. Tycoon Era doesn't. Every progression number is real and stable. Every cost curve is honest. Every reward is earnable through play.

If you want to pay for convenience — automating ads with the Tycoon Pass, removing them entirely, or accelerating with a one-time Starter Pack — those options exist. They make play smoother. They don't gate access to the game.

The game is free. It will stay free. The optional in-app purchases are transparent — no auto-renewing surprise subscriptions, no "first 7 days free" tricks, no manipulative pop-ups.
